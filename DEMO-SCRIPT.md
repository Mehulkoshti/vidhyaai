# 🎬 VidhyaAI — Demo Video Script (HACKHAZARDS '26)

**Target length:** ~2:45–3:00 min · **Narration:** English (show Hindi content on screen) · **Tone:** confident, fast, friendly.

## Before you record (checklist)
- Open the live site **https://vidhya-ai-01.netlify.app** and confirm real content shows (not mock).
- Keep ready: a **photo of handwritten notes** (or a short PDF), the **installed mobile APK** on your phone, and a **phone screen-mirror** (scrcpy / QuickTime / Android screen record).
- **Pre-create one Classroom** (note the join code) so you don't wait during recording.
- Record web with a screen recorder (OBS / Loom / Mac Shift+Cmd+5). Speak clearly; keep each scene tight.
- Have **Hindi** selected in the language dropdown for the "your language" moments.

---

## 🎞️ Scene-by-scene

### 1 · Hook + title — 0:00–0:15
**Screen:** Landing page hero (slow scroll).
**Say:**
> "Students drown in notes and PDFs — and almost every study tool assumes you learn in English. Meet **VidhyaAI**: turn any topic, your notes, or a photo of your textbook into instant study material — in your own language. It runs entirely on **Sarvam AI**, India's own AI stack."

### 2 · The core flow — 0:15–0:45
**Screen:** Click **Open App** → type "Photosynthesis", set language to **Hindi**, mode **Summary**, hit send. Show the Hindi summary appear.
**Say:**
> "I'll ask for a summary of Photosynthesis — in Hindi. Sarvam's multilingual LLM writes clean, structured notes natively in Hindi, not just translated."
**Then:** Tap **🔊 Listen**.
> "And it reads them aloud in a natural Indian voice using Sarvam's Bulbul text-to-speech."

### 3 · Voice + doubts — 0:45–1:00
**Screen:** Switch to **💬 Ask** mode, tap the **🎤 mic**, speak a doubt ("Why is chlorophyll green?"), show it transcribe and answer.
**Say:**
> "You can even ask doubts by voice — Sarvam's Saaras speech-to-text listens, and the AI tutor answers, remembering the whole session."

### 4 · Photo → notes (Sarvam Vision) — 1:00–1:20
**Screen:** In a study mode, attach the **photo of handwritten notes** → generate a summary/quiz.
**Say:**
> "Got messy handwritten notes? Snap a photo. Sarvam Vision reads it with OCR and turns it into clean study material automatically."

### 5 · Test yourself + certificate — 1:20–1:45
**Screen:** Mode **🧪 Mock Test** → answer a few (show the timer) → **Submit** → score screen → enter name → **Download Certificate**.
**Say:**
> "Ready to test yourself? Take a timed mock exam, get a scored report that flags your weak topics, and pass to download a certificate. One tap turns any weak topic into fresh flashcards."

### 6 · More modes + progress — 1:45–2:05
**Screen:** Quickly flash **Flashcards** (flip one), **Mind Map**, and **📖 Course** (a syllabus → chapters). Then open **📊 Progress** and **📚 Saved**.
**Say:**
> "Nine modes in all — flashcards with spaced repetition, mind maps, day-by-day planners, even a full course from a syllabus. Everything saves to your library, and your streak and scores are tracked."

### 7 · Learn together — 2:05–2:30
**Screen:** Go to **Classroom** → show your pre-made class code → open **/classroom/CODE** in a second tab, take it as a student, submit → show the **live leaderboard** update. Briefly show **Study Rooms**.
**Say:**
> "It's not just solo. Teachers create a class quiz with a join code, students take it, and scores appear on a live leaderboard. Study Rooms let a whole group build shared notes together in real time."

### 8 · Mobile app — 2:30–2:45
**Screen:** Phone screen-mirror: open the **VidhyaAI Android app**, generate a summary, tap **Listen**, show voice input.
**Say:**
> "And it's on your phone too — a native Android app built with **Expo**, with the same modes, voice, and read-aloud, all on the same Sarvam backend."

### 9 · Close — 2:45–3:00
**Screen:** Back to landing "Four Sarvam superpowers" section, then the hero.
**Say:**
> "VidhyaAI — LLM, text-to-speech, vision, and speech recognition, all from Sarvam, in twelve Indian languages. Study smarter, in your language. Built for HACKHAZARDS '26. Thank you!"

## Tips
- **Show, don't tell:** keep clicking; let results appear on screen while you narrate.
- **Emphasize the 2 differentiators:** (1) *your language* (Hindi output + voice), (2) *four Sarvam products* working together.
- If over 3 min, cut Scene 6 details or Study Rooms — keep Classroom + mobile.
- End screen: show the live URL + "Powered by Sarvam AI 🇮🇳".
